Selected Work

The Price of Presence

Seeking Publication, 2026

Hushed ruptures in law and technology mean that New Yorkers are automatically tolled for their bodily presence in public: on park benches, on subway platforms, in the fast lane of the sidewalk... A corporate climber who should know better wants to go to court over the twenty dollars he owes. Joining forces with a lawyer out of his depth, the two must break free from the very real purgatory that contains those who run afoul with the system.
